# Eligibility Criteria: PCC Framework

## Population

### Included
- Adults (aged 18 years and above)
- Diagnosis of schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorder, or schizophreniform disorder
- Diagnosis based on standardized criteria:
  - DSM-IV or DSM-5
  - ICD-10 or ICD-11
- Both inpatient and outpatient settings accepted

### Excluded
- Adolescents or pediatric populations (<18 years)
- Clinical high-risk (CHR) for psychosis without formal diagnosis
- Ultra-high risk (UHR) populations without diagnosis
- Other psychotic disorders not in schizophrenia spectrum:
  - Brief psychotic disorder
  - Delusional disorder
  - Psychotic depression
  - Bipolar disorder with psychosis (unless schizoaffective)
- Studies that do not separate schizophrenia spectrum from other diagnoses
- Animal studies

## Concept

### 1. Smoking Status (Exposure)

**Preferred definition:**
- Current smoker: Daily smoking for at least 1 year
- Non-smoker: Never smoked or <100 lifetime cigarettes

**Acceptable definitions:**
- Study-specific criteria with explicit classification
- Self-reported smoking status
- Biochemically verified (cotinine) smoking status

**Categories to extract:**
- Current smokers
- Former smokers (noted separately if available)
- Never smokers
- Non-smokers (former + never combined)

**Smoking severity measures to extract:**
- Cigarettes per day (CPD)
- Pack-years
- Smoking duration (years)
- Fagerström Test for Nicotine Dependence (FTND)
- Time to first cigarette
- Abstinence period before scan

### 2. Structural Brain Measures (Outcome)

**Included - T1-weighted structural MRI (sMRI):**
- Gray matter volume (whole brain, regional, VBM)
- Cortical thickness
- Cortical surface area
- Subcortical volumes (hippocampus, amygdala, basal ganglia, thalamus)
- White matter volume (structural, not diffusion-based)
- Total brain volume
- Intracranial volume
- Ventricular volumes
- Cerebellar volumes

**Excluded imaging modalities:**
- Functional MRI (fMRI) - task-based or resting-state
- Diffusion tensor imaging (DTI) / Diffusion-weighted imaging (DWI)
- Positron emission tomography (PET)
- Single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT)
- Magnetic resonance spectroscopy (MRS)
- Electroencephalography (EEG)
- Magnetoencephalography (MEG)

**Analysis approaches accepted:**
- Voxel-based morphometry (VBM)
- Region of interest (ROI) analysis
- Surface-based analysis (FreeSurfer)
- Manual tracing
- Automated segmentation

### 3. Psychiatric Symptom Severity

**Validated scales to extract:**
- Positive and Negative Syndrome Scale (PANSS)
  - Positive subscale
  - Negative subscale
  - General psychopathology subscale
  - Total score
- Brief Psychiatric Rating Scale (BPRS)
- Scale for Assessment of Positive Symptoms (SAPS)
- Scale for Assessment of Negative Symptoms (SANS)
- Clinical Global Impression (CGI)
- Global Assessment of Functioning (GAF)

**Correlations of interest:**
- Brain structure × Symptom severity
- Differential correlations in smokers vs. non-smokers
- Mediation/moderation analyses

### 4. Psychological Flexibility (Novel Extension)

**Measures to extract if reported:**
- Acceptance and Action Questionnaire II (AAQ-II)
- Cognitive Fusion Questionnaire (CFQ)
- Valued Living Questionnaire (VLQ)
- Other ACT-related measures

**Expected: Very few or no studies will report this - documenting this gap is a key objective**

## Context

### Study Designs Included
- Cross-sectional studies
- Longitudinal/cohort studies
- Case-control studies
- Baseline data from intervention studies (RCTs)

### Study Designs Excluded
- Systematic reviews and meta-analyses (used for reference screening only)
- Narrative reviews
- Case reports/case series (<10 participants)
- Conference abstracts without full-text
- Protocols without results
- Letters/editorials without original data

### Publication Criteria
- Peer-reviewed publications
- Published in English language
- No date restrictions (from database inception)
- Full-text available

### Geographic/Setting
- Any country or setting
- Both high-income and low/middle-income countries

## Summary Table

| Criterion | Include | Exclude |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Population** | Schizophrenia, schizoaffective, schizophreniform (adults) | CHR, UHR, other psychoses, pediatric |
| **Imaging** | T1-weighted sMRI (GM volume, cortical thickness, etc.) | fMRI, DTI, PET, SPECT, MRS |
| **Smoking** | Studies comparing smokers vs. non-smokers | No smoking comparison |
